Empowering students through self-paced learning and strong partnerships.Self-study is a method of learning where students take charge of their own studying outside of the classroom, without direct supervision. This allows the student to take control of how, what, when, and where they learn. This method of studying is preferred by independent learners because they can take learning into their own hands. They assess what information they need, find the resources to supplement their learning, and complete their assessments at their own pace. Anyone can incorporate self-studying in their own learning practice, not just independent learners. Some researchers consider kids with complex needs a good fit for the self-study learning method as well, with some minor adjustments to better fit their individual needs. About Us

Located in the serene hills of Himachal Pradesh, NavGurukul's Himachal Campus is a unique initiative focusing on self-study and peer-learning for BCA students. Our campus promotes independence, critical thinking, and collaborative learning in a peaceful environment.

Academically, the university is organised into seven colleges: Dr. Kehm Singh Gill Akal College of Agriculture, Akal College of Engineering and Technology, Akal College of Basic Sciences, Akal College of Health and Allied Sciences, Akal College of Education, Akal College of Economics, Commerce and Management, and Akal College of Arts and Social Sciences. Within each college are a number of departments, offering both undergraduate and graduate degree programmes. There are a range of clubs and societies on offer for students to join such as the English literary society, cultural club, sports club, and renewable energy club. The university's research projects focus on the fields of crop improvement, food processing, renewable energy, biodiversity conservation, nanotechnology, microbial biotechnology, herbal products and drugs from medicinal plants.
